From Playground Hustles to Global Business: My Journey to Freelancing

Growing up in Hungary, where I was born in Germany, my childhood was shaped by humble beginnings and a resourceful spirit. Even as a young child, I displayed a knack for entrepreneurship. I vividly remember making and selling coloring sheets and booklets on the playground, an early sign of my sales savvy and creative problem-solving skills. This youthful hustle set the stage for a life defined by adaptability and innovation.

Navigating the diverse cultures of my early life, I quickly learned the value of adapting to new environments. Moving from Germany to Hungary, and then later to the UK for work, I embraced each new culture and language with enthusiasm. This ability to adapt and thrive in different settings has been a cornerstone of my personal and professional growth.

After completing my education and gaining valuable experience working in the UK, I began to see the potential for broader horizons. My career took shape in various roles, from client delivery to sales development, where I honed my skills in building relationships, strategizing market entry, and optimizing sales processes. Each role reinforced my belief in the power of tailored strategies and the importance of understanding local markets—a belief I carry into my freelancing today.

Travel has been a major influence on my life. I've had the privilege of exploring most continents solo, with a particular focus on Colombia and Mexico. These experiences have not only broadened my worldview but have also deepened my appreciation for diverse cultures and perspectives. They have fueled my passion for connecting people and businesses across borders.

Venturing into freelancing was a natural next step for me. I wanted to combine my extensive experience in sales and market expansion with my love for travel and cultural exchange. As a freelancer, I now have the freedom to help companies navigate the DACH market, leveraging my unique insights and multilingual skills to drive growth and success.

Each chapter of my journey—whether it was hustling on the playground or navigating international markets—has contributed to the person I am today. My goal is to use my diverse experiences to assist businesses in achieving their expansion goals, while continuing to explore and learn from the world around me.
